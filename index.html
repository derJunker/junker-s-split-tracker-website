Junker's Split Tracker

Level up your Pogostuck Speedrunning expierience.

Features

  • Default Pogostuck Split-Tracker Functionality
  • Gold Splits
  • Gold Paces
  • Pace Estimations
  • Sum of Best
  • Race your best times
  • Reset Counters
  • Custom Modes
  • Loot Seed display
  • More Customization (coloring, hide skipped splits, size)
  • Reversed UD Splits

Limitations

  • Only works with borderless not fullscreen
  • Only tested on Windows
  • Anti-Virus programs might give a warning when installing :)

About

Hey :). Thanks for checking out my split tracker :D. I put a lot of effort into it and i hope you like it.

If you play borderless you can use it just like the default split timer ingame just with more features. It allows you to play custom modes like Boostless with their own split times.

If you play with fullscreen there is currently no way for me to overlay the tracker. However you can let it run on a second monitor, or just in the background to track your gold splits.

I hope you like it and feedback is greatly appreciated! The program is completely free to use, however if you want to support me you can choose to do so here.
